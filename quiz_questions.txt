PHISHING AWARENESS QUIZ

1. What is phishing?
a) A gaming attack
b) A cyber attack to steal sensitive information
c) A file compression method
Answer: b

2. Which of the following is a sign of a phishing email?
a) Trusted sender address
b) Proper grammar always
c) Urgent message asking for personal details
Answer: c

3. What should you do before clicking a link in an email?
a) Click immediately
b) Ignore the sender
c) Verify the link and sender
Answer: c

4. What is smishing?
a) Phishing through SMS
b) Phishing through video
c) Phishing through printer
Answer: a

5. What is one good way to stay safe from phishing?
a) Share passwords
b) Enable two-factor authentication
c) Click unknown attachments
Answer: b
